Binabasalan Islet:

"Welcome to Binabasalan Islet, a marine protected paradise nestled in the western part of Salcedo Municipality, adjacent to Barangay Maliwaliw, within the enchanting embrace of Leyte Gulf. This pristine island boasts fine white sand, vibrant coral reefs, and a diverse array of sea creatures. Covering approximately 3 hectares, it's a haven for snorkeling and scuba diving enthusiasts. Accessible via a scenic 45-minute motorized pumpboat ride from Poblacion Salcedo, the journey to Binabasalan Islet is as breathtaking as the destination itself."

More About Salcedo

The municipality of Salcedo is at Eastern Samar in Region VIII with a land area of 113.68 square kilometers (sq km).
It is composed of 41 barangays, with 26 coastal barangays and 2 island barangays.

Privacy Policy

Salcedo is a 5th class municipality within the province of Eastern Samar, Philippines. This privacy policy will explain how we use the personal data we collect from you when you use our website. We are committed to protect and respect your personal data privacy. Our use of your data is subject to Republic Act No. 10173 also known as the “Data Privacy Act”.

What data do we collect?

We collect the following data:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Contact Number

How do we collect your data?

You directly provide us with the data we collect when you:

  • Send a message to us via the “Contact Us” section of the Website
  • Send an email to us

We may also receive your data indirectly from the following sources:

  • Facebook
  • Instagram

How will we use your data?

We collect your data so that we can:

  • Immediately contact you in case there are concerns during disasters
  • Email you with responses

How do we store your data?

We securely store your data through our email account which can only be accessed by duly authorized persons. We keep your data indefinitely unless sooner removed or deleted by a proper government action, or when you request for data erasure.

What are your data protection rights?

We would like to make sure that you are fully aware of all your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:

  • Right to access – You have the right to request for copies of your personal data
  • Right to rectification – You have the right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request that we complete any information you believe is incomplete.
  • Right to erasure – You have the right to request that we erase your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • Right to restrict processing – You have the right to request that we restrict processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • Right to object to processing – You have the right to object about processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • Right to data portability – You have the right to request that we transfer the data we collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.

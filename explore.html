FEATURED TOURIST ATTRACTION

Minamhay Sandbar:

"Step into the enchanting world of Minamhay Sandbar, a pristine stretch of pure white sand embraced by the sea. This magical sandbar reveals its glory during low tide, glistening and miraculously intact as the tide recedes. Despite its modest size, it offers a complete island experience, perfect for captivating photographs. Located just a 30-minute boat ride from Poblacion Salcedo and a mere 10 minutes away from Binabasalan Island, the journey to this captivating sandbar is as delightful as the experience itself."

Beaches:

Sink your toes into the soft, powdery sand of our pristine beaches. Feel the gentle ocean breeze, and witness the spectacular sunsets that paint the sky in vivid hues.

Yolanda
Beach

Description: A 1.41km stretch of fine, white sand known locally as "Yolanda Beach".
Location: Brgy. Jagnaya, Salcedo, Eastern Samar
Travel time: 10-minute ride via motorcycle/car from Brgy. Bagtong waiting shed.

Alog
Beach

Description: A 2.1km stretch of long, fine, white sand perfect for swimming, family bonding, outing and camping.
Location: Brgy. Alog, Salcedo, Eastern Samar
Travel time: 10-minute ride via motorcycle/car from Brgy. Bagtong waiting shed.

Islands & Islets:

Embark on island-hopping adventures to explore the untouched beauty of our islands and islets. Each one is a unique paradise waiting to be discovered.

Caves and Lagoons:

Venture into the mysterious depths of our caves and lagoons, where the natural wonders of underground landscapes await your exploration.

Heritage Stairs to Bukid Koykoyon:

Don't miss the chance to step back in time and visit one of our historical treasures, the "Heritage Stairs of Bukid Koykoyon," which narrates a tale of resilience and heritage.

Buabua Miraculous Church:

Explore the architectural marvel and spiritual significance of the "Buabua Miraculous Church." This historical site stands as a testament to faith and miracles, offering a unique perspective on Salcedo's religious heritage.

More About Salcedo

The municipality of Salcedo is at Eastern Samar in Region VIII with a land area of 113.68 square kilometers (sq km).
It is composed of 41 barangays, with 26 coastal barangays and 2 island barangays.

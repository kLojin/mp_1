Minamhay Sandbar:

This echanted white sand bar is embraced with finely stretched of pure sand, surrounded by the sea. This Islet only appears druing low tide while as the tide recedes, a glistening sanbar appears and it still amazingly intact. It’s a mere sandbar but definitely, it will give youan island experience, very perfect for picture taking which seemed to be cast away in an island.The Sandbar is a 30-minute drive via motorized boat from Pobalcion Salcedo and about 10-minute away from Binabasalan island.

Islands & Islets:

Embark on island-hopping adventures to explore the untouched beauty of our islands and islets. Each one is a unique paradise waiting to be discovered.

Caves and Lagoons:

Venture into the mysterious depths of our caves and lagoons, where the natural wonders of underground landscapes await your exploration.

Heritage Stairs to Bukid Koykoyon:

Don't miss the chance to step back in time and visit one of our historical treasures, the "Heritage Stairs of Bukid Koykoyon," which narrates a tale of resilience and heritage.

Buabua Miraculous Church:

Explore the architectural marvel and spiritual significance of the "Buabua Miraculous Church." This historical site stands as a testament to faith and miracles, offering a unique perspective on Salcedo's religious heritage.

More About Salcedo

The municipality of Salcedo is at Eastern Samar in Region VIII with a land area of 113.68 square kilometers (sq km).
It is composed of 41 barangays, with 26 coastal barangays and 2 island barangays.

We would like to make sure that you are fully aware of all your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:

  • Right to access – You have the right to request for copies of your personal data
  • Right to rectification – You have the right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request that we complete any information you believe is incomplete.
  • Right to erasure – You have the right to request that we erase your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • Right to restrict processing – You have the right to request that we restrict processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • Right to object to processing – You have the right to object about processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  • Right to data portability – You have the right to request that we transfer the data we collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.
